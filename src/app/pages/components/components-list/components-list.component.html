Material List
Two-Line List with Avatars
<mat-list>

Lists are made up of a continuous column of rows. Each row contains a tile. Primary actions fill the tile, and supplemental actions are represented by icons and text.

Example:
<mat-list>
<mat-list-item>
<img mat-list-avatar src="avatar.png">
<h3 matLine>John</h3>
<p matLine>Brunch?</p>
<mat-list-item>
</mat-list>

John

Brunch? -- Did you want to go on Sunday? I was thinking

Peter

Summer BBQ -- Wish I could come, but I have some special

Nancy

Oui oui -- Have you booked the Paris trip?

Three-Line List with Avatars

Lists with three lines add extra content to distinguish your list-item.

Example:
<mat-list>
<mat-list-item>
<img mat-list-avatar src="avatar.png">
<h3 matLine>John</h3>
<p matLine>Brunch?</p>
<p matLine class="subline">
Did you want to go on Sunday?
</p>
<mat-list-item>
</mat-list>
